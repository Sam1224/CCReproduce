When Rules Fall Short: Agent-Driven Discovery of Emerging Content Issues in Short Video Platforms
89
TikTok Inc. · arXiv:2601.11634 · Jan 2026
Content Governance LLM Agent Clustering Multimodal
Method Overview
Short-video platforms face rapidly emerging content issues that outpace manual policy discovery. AgentIssueDiscovery proposes a 3-stage pipeline: (1) A multimodal LLM Agent recalls videos potentially containing new issues by comparing against existing policies; (2) Two-stage clustering groups recalled videos into coarse clusters, then fine-grained sub-clusters to distinguish variants vs. new issues; (3) Annotation policies are auto-generated from clusters and queued for human review. Deployed at TikTok, achieving F1 +20% and ~15% reduction in problematic video views.
短视频平台新兴内容问题涌现速度超过人工发现能力。AgentIssueDiscovery 提出三阶段 pipeline： (1) 多模态 LLM Agent 召回可能包含新问题的视频； (2) 两阶段聚类区分已知变体与全新子问题； (3) 自动生成标注策略供人工审核。 已在 TikTok 实际部署，F1 提升 >20%，违规视频曝光减少约 15%。
Innovation / 创新分析
First systematic automated framework for emerging issue discovery in video platforms. Two-stage clustering novel: coarse groups + fine discrimination of variant vs. new. LLM multimodal reasoning outperforms keyword/rule approaches. Closed-loop policy iteration.
首个系统化新兴内容问题自动发现框架；两阶段聚类区分变体与新问题；多模态 LLM 超越规则方法；策略迭代闭环。

E-VAds: An E-commerce Short Videos Understanding Benchmark for MLLMs
84
Alibaba / Taobao · arXiv:2602.08355 · Feb 2026
E-commerce MLLM Benchmark Multi-Agent
Method Overview
E-commerce short videos (e.g., Taobao product demos) require understanding commercial intent, a dimension absent from general video benchmarks. E-VAds contributes: (1) 3,961 Taobao videos + 19,785 open-ended Q&A pairs covering product ID, commercial intent, marketing strategy; (2) A multimodal information density framework showing e-commerce videos are significantly denser than general video; (3) E-VAds-R1: multi-agent annotation + RL fine-tuning (GRPO) achieving 109.2% improvement in commercial intent reasoning with only a few hundred samples.
电商短视频需要理解商业意图，现有通用基准不覆盖此维度。E-VAds 提供： (1) 淘宝 3961 条视频 + 19785 个开放式 Q&A； (2) 信息密度量化框架（电商视频密度显著更高）； (3) E-VAds-R1：多智能体标注+RL微调，商业意图推理提升 109.2%，仅用数百样本。
Innovation / 创新分析
First benchmark for e-commerce short video MLLM evaluation. Information density framework objectively quantifies domain complexity. Multi-agent annotation scales labeling. Few-shot RL fine-tuning (109.2% gain) shows remarkable sample efficiency.

TIGER-FG: Text-Guided Implicit Fine-Grained Grounding for E-commerce Retrieval
83
arXiv:2605.18434 · May 2026 ✦ May 19 listing
E-commerce Retrieval Visual Grounding Distillation
Method Overview
E-commerce IMMR (Implicit Multi-step Multi-modal Retrieval): user uploads a crop image (e.g., a watch detail shot), retrieve the full product page (full image + structured text). TIGER-FG uses text attributes as semantic guidance in a cross-attention over image patches — no explicit object detection required. Combined with Dual Distillation: spatial-relational distillation (patch-level attention from box priors) and similarity-distribution distillation (soft targets across batch).
电商图搜 IMMR 任务：用户上传局部目标图，检索完整商品页。 TIGER-FG 用商品文本属性作为语义引导，对全图 patch 做 cross-attention 聚合， 无需显式目标检测。配合双蒸馏策略（空间关系蒸馏+相似度分布蒸馏），端到端实现细粒度检索。
Innovation / 创新分析
Novel IMMR task definition. Text-semantic guided implicit grounding avoids error-propagating two-stage detection. Dual distillation provides supervision without box labels. Online-serving friendly (no detection overhead).
IMMR 任务定义新颖；文本引导隐式 Grounding 避免显式检测误差传播；双蒸馏在无框标注时提供有效监督；在线服务友好。

CVA: Compressed Video Aggregator for Efficient Micro-Video Recommendation
79
arXiv:2605.08810 · May 9, 2026
Video Recommendation VFM Short Video
Method Overview
CVA addresses the efficiency bottleneck of integrating Video Foundation Models (VFMs) into micro-video recommendation. Key ideas: (1) Freeze VFM, train lightweight aggregator only to decouple content understanding from preference learning; (2) CLIP + title-guided semantic keyframe resampling to reduce frame redundancy; (3) Latent reasoning aggregation without cross-attention projection → compact video embeddings. Consistent gains on MicroLens & Short-Video with orders-of-magnitude reduction in GPU memory & training time.
CVA 解决 VFM 集成到微视频推荐的效率瓶颈。核心：(1) 冻结 VFM，只训练轻量聚合器； (2) CLIP+标题引导语义关键帧重采样；(3) 无 cross-attention 的隐式推理聚合。 在 MicroLens 和 Short-Video 上持续提升效果，训练时间/显存降低数量级。

GRE-MC: Robust Multimodal Recommendation via Graph Retrieval-Enhanced Modality Completion
70
National University of Singapore · arXiv:2605.00670 · May 2026
Recommendation Multimodal Graph
Method Overview
Real-world multimodal recommendation data suffers from modality incompleteness (sensor failures, annotation scarcity). GRE-MC: (1) Modality-aware subgraph retrieval from the full user-item graph to provide rich context; (2) Graph Transformer jointly encodes query node + retrieved subgraph via global attention to complete missing features; (3) Learnable sparse-routing codebook regularizes latent embeddings for robustness.
GRE-MC 解决多模态推荐中的模态缺失问题。模态感知子图检索提供丰富上下文，图 Transformer 全局注意力联合编码补全缺失模态，可学习稀疏路由码本正则化嵌入提升鲁棒性。
